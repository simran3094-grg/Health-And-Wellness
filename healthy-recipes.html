ü•ë Avocado Toast

A quick and healthy breakfast option with creamy avocado and crunchy toast.

Ingredients:

  • 1 ripe avocado
  • 2 slices of whole grain bread
  • Olive oil
  • Salt & pepper

Instructions:

1. Toast the bread slices until golden brown.
2. Mash the avocado and spread it over the toast.
3. Drizzle with olive oil and sprinkle with salt and pepper to taste.

ü•ó Quinoa Salad

A fresh and nutritious salad packed with protein, perfect for lunch.

Ingredients:

  • 1 cup quinoa
  • 1 cucumber, diced
  • 1 cup cherry tomatoes, halved
  • 1/4 cup olive oil
  • Juice of 1 lemon
  • Salt & pepper to taste

Instructions:

1. Cook quinoa as per package instructions and let it cool.
2. In a large bowl, combine the quinoa with cucumber, tomatoes, and olive oil.
3. Drizzle with lemon juice and season with salt and pepper. Mix well and serve.

üçì Smoothie Bowl

A refreshing smoothie bowl topped with fruits and granola, perfect for breakfast.

Ingredients:

  • 1 banana
  • 1/2 cup mixed berries
  • 1/2 cup yogurt
  • 1/4 cup granola
  • Honey (optional)

Instructions:

1. Blend the banana, berries, and yogurt until smooth.
2. Pour into a bowl and top with granola and a drizzle of honey.

üçØ Chia Pudding

Prepare this simple yet delicious chia pudding for a healthy snack or dessert.

Ingredients:

  • 2 tbsp chia seeds
  • 1/2 cup almond milk
  • 1 tsp vanilla extract
  • Honey or maple syrup (optional)

Instructions:

1. Mix chia seeds, almond milk, and vanilla in a bowl.
2. Refrigerate for at least 4 hours or overnight until it thickens.
3. Top with your favorite fruits and serve.

üçó Grilled Chicken Salad

A healthy and delicious salad featuring lean protein from grilled chicken.

Ingredients:

  • 2 boneless chicken breasts
  • 1 cup mixed greens
  • 1/4 cup feta cheese
  • 1/4 cup balsamic vinaigrette
  • 1 cucumber, sliced

Instructions:

1. Grill the chicken breasts until fully cooked.
2. Slice the chicken and set aside.
3. Toss the greens, cucumber, and feta in a bowl.
4. Add the chicken and drizzle with balsamic vinaigrette. Mix well.
