Mental Health Awareness

What is Mental Health?

Mental health includes our emotional, psychological, and social well-being. It affects how we think, feel, and act, and it can also influence how we handle stress, relate to others, and make choices. Mental health is important at every stage of life, from childhood and adolescence through adulthood.

Common Mental Health Conditions

Several common mental health conditions affect people worldwide. Below are some examples:

  • Depression
  • Anxiety Disorders
  • Obsessive-Compulsive Disorder (OCD)
  • Bipolar Disorder
  • Post-Traumatic Stress Disorder (PTSD)

Strategies for Caregivers

Taking care of a loved one with a mental health condition can be challenging. Here are some strategies for caregivers:

  • Learn about the condition
  • Provide emotional support and understanding
  • Encourage treatment and therapy
  • Set boundaries to prevent caregiver burnout
